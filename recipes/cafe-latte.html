Cafe Latte

Description

Learn how to make a cafe latte with this easy recipe that will please all fellow coffee drinkers. I love cafe lattes but buying them can be pricey. Try this at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 â…“ cups hot freshly brewed dark roast espresso coffee

Steps

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan set over medium-low heat. Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam.
  2. Brew espresso and pour into four cups. Pour in milk, holding back the foam with a spoon. Spoon foam over the top.

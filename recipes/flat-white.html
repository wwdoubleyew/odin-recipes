Flat White Coffee

Description

A coffee drink with espresso, steamed milk and a layer of foam on top. It is similar to a latte and cappuccino, but has a thinner layer of foam on top. Modern coffee shops have very small differences between these three coffee drinks, and often times the lines are blurred.

Ingredients

  • 2 espresso shots (2 ounces)
  • 4 ounces steamed milk

Steps

  1. Prepare for the flat white
  2. Make the espresso
  3. Steam the milk
